Frequently Asked Questions

What is DarkSmog AI WhatsApp chatbot?

DarkSmog AI provides intelligent WhatsApp chatbots for businesses to automate customer interactions, handle FAQs, capture leads, and enhance customer support.

How does the chatbot help my business?

Our chatbot can handle customer queries, automate lead generation, manage orders, schedule appointments, and provide 24/7 support, saving you time and resources.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a free plan with basic chatbot features so you can test it before upgrading to a paid plan for advanced functionalities.

How do I integrate the chatbot with my business?

Simply sign up, configure your chatbot, and connect it to your WhatsApp Business account using Twilio. Our team can also assist with setup.

Can I customize the chatbot replies?

Yes! You can fully customize responses, automate workflows, and even train the chatbot to match your business needs.
